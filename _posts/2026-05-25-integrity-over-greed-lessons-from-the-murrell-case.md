## Wisdom Teaching
*Concept*: "The gentleman understands what is moral. The small man understands what is profitable." – Confucius, Analects 4.16
When entrusted with responsibility, choosing personal gain over ethical duty erodes trust and damages community. True leadership requires putting righteousness above profit.
Let us remember that integrity is the foundation of all meaningful relationships and institutions.
*概念*: "君子义以为上，小人利以为上。" – 孔子，《论语》4.16
当人受托责任时，以私利代替道德义务会侵蚀信任并损害社群。真正的领导力要求将正义置于利润之上。
让我们记住，诚信是所有有意义关系和制度的基石。

## Reflection
The Murrell case reveals how even those in positions of trust can succumb to the temptation of personal gain. Confucian wisdom reminds us that true character is revealed not in times of abundance, but in how we handle responsibility when no one is watching. By cultivating inner integrity, we create societies where trust can flourish and institutions can serve the common good rather than individual interests.
默雷尔案例表明，即使是受信任的人也可能屈服于私利的诱惑。儒家智慧提醒我们，真正的品格不在于丰裕时刻的表现，而在于无人监督时如何履行责任。通过培养内在诚信，我们能创造信任得以繁荣、制度服务于共同利益而非私利的社会。
