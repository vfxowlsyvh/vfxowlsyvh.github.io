##  Wisdom from Quaker | Quaker智慧
*Concept*: "Walk cheerfully over the world, answering that of God in everyone."
This Quaker principle encourages seeing the divine in every person, promoting equality and peace. In the context of border checks, it reminds us to treat all travelers with dignity and compassion, reducing unnecessary barriers that cause suffering.
May we seek to simplify our borders and extend kindness to all who cross them.
